EMPIRE Stock Selection Framework — The 6-Filter Quality Gate
E
Earnings Quality
  • PAT growing >20% CAGR for 3+ years
  • Operating Cash Flow ≥ Net Profit
  • ROCE > 20%
  • Zero or minimal debt (D/E < 0.3)
M
Moat Strength
  • Pricing power (can raise prices?)
  • Brand, switching costs, network effects
  • Consistent or expanding margins over cycles
P
Promoter Integrity
  • Promoter holding >50% and increasing
  • Zero pledging (red flag if >10%)
  • Good capital allocation history
I
Industry Tailwind
  • Riding a decade-long structural theme
  • Capex, Premiumization, Financialization
  • Digital, Defence, PLI beneficiaries
R
Runway (TAM)
  • Can 5x revenue in 10 years?
  • Underpenetrated sector preferred
  • Insurance, Wealth, QSR, Hospitals
E
Entry Price
  • PEG ratio < 1.5 ideal for growth
  • Buy on 20–30% corrections in quality
  • Reasonable — not necessarily cheap
